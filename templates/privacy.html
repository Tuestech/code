Tuesday Technologies Privacy Policy

Tuesday Technologies develops web applications for personal use. “Tuesday” or “we” or “us” or “our” refers to Tuesday Technologies in this privacy policy.

This privacy policy applies whenever you access or interact with any of our products, services, and/or content.

Information we collect

To provide our products, services, and content, we must collect data. Information we collect includes the name, start date, due date, progress, and link per tasks entered by the user. We also collect user preferences like the progress curve and convenient access links.

We also identify unique users by their Google accounts. We will never request any personal information such as name, age, location, etc. Every user is assigned a unique “user identification number,” which we use to identify users.

How we collect information

Information associated with tasks and links is collected directly from the user through text input boxes with appropriate labels. Information associated with user preferences is also collected directly from the user, but through buttons with appropriate labels.

Information to identify the user is collected through Google OAuth. Users are presented with the Google “Oauth Consent Screen” where they are asked to log in and give consent to provide identification information.

How we use collected information

Information associated with tasks is shown directly to the user. The start date, due date, and progress associated with tasks, and the user’s preferences about progress curves are used to calculate the difference between the user’s current progress and the predicted progress.

Information collected through Google OAuth is used to identify users from each other. We cannot read any information associated with individual users without their consent.

Individual user information, such as information associated with tasks and user identification, is never shared with any entities not described in this privacy policy.

Data retention

We must retain the data we collect in order to provide our products, services, and content. Data will be retained until the user presses a “delete” button on any associated task or a “delete all data” button associated with all user data.

Communications

When this privacy policy is changed, it will be updated. Users will be able to view changes to the privacy policy at https://tues.tech/privacy.

Dispute resolution

Users who do not accept this privacy policy cannot use or consume our products, services, and content. Entities who want more information about how this policy should be interpreted may contact us with the information listed below.
