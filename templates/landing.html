Tuesday helps you take control over your work, effortlessly manage your time, and build executive functioning skills, all for free.

Who we are

We are value transparency, utility, and accessibility. That means there will never be any ads or pricetags on any of our products. Everything will always be made with the interests of students in mind, and we will open source as much as possible.

Work Management

We will help you get things done. Customize your dashboard to show only the information you need. You will have smart algorithms, insightful visualizations, and research-proven techniques, all in one place.

Data analysis

Working with data can be hard, but it doesn't have to be. You can design and train advanced neural networks to fit your needs, all without writing a single line of code.

Support us

If you want to get involved or just want to support our work, that's great! We aren't accepting donations, but you can still contribute. Feedback, insights, and suggestions are invaluable to us and would be greatly appreciated.
