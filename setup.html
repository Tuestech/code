What is a progress curve?



The main way our sorting algorithm knows how to prioritize your tasks is by checking against your selected progress curve. A progress curve is a measure of the amount of a task completed for a time during the duration of that task. Options 1 and 3 will have you complete more of a task up front, with Option 3 being more aggressive. Option 2 will have you complete an equal amount every day for the duration of the task.
